Welcome to PlaceMap! Before you play, you should know that this experience requires players to only use their phones within the premises of this app.That means no social media, no calls, no form of individual phone use during the meal. We want you to focus on your food and on each other’s company.

UNDERSTOOD

When you start playing, you’ll see a small circle on the top-left corner of the screen, with a number in it. That number signals the amount of players connected to the experience.

UNDERSTOOD

If someone closes the app to make individual use of their phone, all players will get this alert. That way, the group can make sure all players stay true to the social pact.

UNDERSTOOD

If you really need to use your phone for an individual purpose, there is a mechanism for you to do it without breaking the social pact. Just click on the top-left circle and a pop-up screen will appear.

UNDERSTOOD

Then, press the button on the pop-up, and your individual bubble will be created. You’ll then be free to get out of the app without triggering an alert on other players’ phones. But note that they will still know you took some time off as you will be deducted from the number on the top-left circle.
